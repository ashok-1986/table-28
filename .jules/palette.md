## 2026-05-22 - [Tactile Feedback & Accessibility for Critical CTAs]
**Learning:** For critical external-link buttons (like reviews), providing immediate visual feedback ("Redirecting...") and tactile response (active state scaling) significantly reduces double-clicking and user uncertainty during the handoff to another site.
**Action:** Always include a loading/redirecting state for external platform redirects and ensure ARIA labels explicitly state the action.
